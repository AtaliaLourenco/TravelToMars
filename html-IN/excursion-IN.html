Excursions to Mars

INFORMATION:

The Excursions to Mars programs are fully paid, and their values vary according to the chosen itinerary. Each payer will have the experience of living on Mars temporarily for a period of 1 year, with no obligation to work or research. However, they must contribute and follow the rules imposed by the colony, any conflict or mistake made during the tour can lead to fines or imprisonment. It is very important that no settlers are harassed, or removed from their activities by payers. This experience must be lived responsibly. Cameras will be provided during the tour so that you can record videos and take pictures, however at the end of the tours the cameras will be confiscated for photo analysis and will be returned as soon as you have authorization for disclosure.
