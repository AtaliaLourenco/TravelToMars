Mars Colonization Programs

Work Program

The Work Program is dedicated to manual labor, building living blocks, habitat maintenance, organizing and distributing meals, any ordinary person can live and work on Mars as part of this program, the selection process is totally different, during the selection candidates are subjected to various psychological and resistance tests. The Work Program can be temporary or permanent, and each one has a different selection process. The temporary program foresees that the worker will live and work on Mars for 10 years, where the main objective is the general work force. The force work will contribute so that each one has an objective in the colony, and that they can use their skills cooperatively. For this program, you need houses that can also procreate and help populate Mars. Each settler will have their colony where they are obliged to help the settlers with their daily difficulties and meet the objective of their job. For this program, it foresees that any action that could harm the colony will have a trial where they can be sentenced to death, or total isolation of the colonies.

Why did we choose Mars?

  • Water on Mars

    It is possible to extract liquid water from ice stored inside the ground, and oxygen can be generated from carbon dioxide that makes up most of the atmosphere on Mars.

  • Future of Human Race

    Colonies on Mars are able to permanently maintain our population, they provide our species with the ultimate life expectancy. Living on Mars provides another backup plan for the future of the human race.

  • Planetary Exploration

    As on Earth, Mars has plenty metallic oxides, such as iron, silicon and aluminum and with the right technology, it is possible to exploit these resources and develop new technologies.
