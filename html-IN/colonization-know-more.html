Colonization Program

The Colonization Program is totally different from other programs, it is dedicated to the multiplication of human life on Mars, in this program families or young couples can apply, the process is simplified, the fertility of couples and their ability to live in small groups will be analyzed . For children, Mars has a whole structure with schools for life to develop with knowledge. The Colonization Program on Mars can only be permanent, and the colonists once installed on Mars will not have the option of returning to Earth. The program foresees that each house has the obligation to procreate with a minimum of 2 inhabitants during a lifetime. I also have to undergo various fertility tests on a regular basis. Each colonist will have to respond to their president and the obligation to work or develop some activity that will contribute to the colonies in general. For this program, it foresees that any colonist who give up the program while on Mars will be sentenced to death. Each child born on Mars belongs to a colony, meaning, will never be able to leave Mars, because it is not known how the organism of two Martians will return to other planets or to Earth.

Why did we choose Mars?

  • Water on Mars

    It is possible to extract liquid water from ice stored inside the ground, and oxygen can be generated from carbon dioxide that makes up most of the atmosphere on Mars.

  • Future of Human Race

    Colonies on Mars are able to permanently maintain our population, they provide our species with the ultimate life expectancy. Living on Mars provides another backup plan for the future of the human race.

  • Planetary Exploration

    As on Earth, Mars has plenty metallic oxides, such as iron, silicon and aluminum and with the right technology, it is possible to exploit these resources and develop new technologies.
